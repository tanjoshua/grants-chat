Singapore Government Grants

Welcome to the Singapore Government Grants Information Portal. This page provides information about various government grants available for businesses and individuals in Singapore.

Featured Business Grants

Productivity Solutions Grant (PSG)

The Productivity Solutions Grant (PSG) supports businesses in the adoption of productivity solutions and equipment. It covers sector-specific solutions including retail, food, logistics, precision engineering, construction, and landscaping.

Grant Amount: Up to 70% funding support

Learn More

Enterprise Development Grant (EDG)

The Enterprise Development Grant supports projects that help Singapore companies grow and transform. This grant supports projects under three pillars: Core Capabilities, Innovation and Productivity, and Market Access.

Grant Amount: Up to 70% of qualifying costs

Learn More

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for most government grants, businesses must:

  • Be registered and operating in Singapore
  • Have at least 30% local shareholding
  • Be in a financially viable position to start and complete the project

Application Process

Step Description
1. Check Eligibility Verify if your business meets the eligibility criteria for the grant.
2. Prepare Documents Gather necessary documents including business profile, financial statements, and project proposal.
3. Submit Application Submit your application through the Business Grants Portal.
4. Evaluation Your application will be evaluated by the relevant government agency.
5. Outcome Notification You will be notified of the outcome of your application.
